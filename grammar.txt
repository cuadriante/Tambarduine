
PARSER:

expr            : RESERVED VAR ASSIGN expr 
                | arith-expr

arith-expr      : arith-expression (PLUS|MINUS) term
                | term

term            : term (TIMES|DIVIDE|WHOLEDIVIDE|MODULE|POWER) factor
                | factor

factor          :NUMBER
                | VAR
                | LPAREN factor RPAREN

--------------------------------------------

COPY:

expr            : RESERVED VAR ASSIGN expr 
                | arith-expr

arith-expr      : arith-expression (PLUS|MINUS) term
                | arith-expression (EQUALS|DIFFERENT|LESSTHAN|MORETHAN|LESSTHANE|MORETHANE) term
                | NEGATIVE arith-expression
                | term

term            : term (TIMES|DIVIDE|WHOLEDIVIDE|MODULE|POWER) factor
                | factor

factor          :NUMBER
                | VAR
                | LPAREN factor RPAREN
